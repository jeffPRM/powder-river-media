Jeff Sellenrick

Jeff Sellenrick has been a professional photographer since 2012. Over more than a decade of shooting weddings, portraits and headshots, he built a discipline around work where there are no second chances — where the light, the moment, and the detail have to be right the first time.

Powder River Media grew from a deliberate decision: real estate agents are some of the most relationship-driven professionals in any market. They work hard to build trust with their clients, they rely on a network that notices the details, and when they find a service they can count on, they come back. Jeff built Powder River Media to be that company — the media partner agents call for every listing, refer to their colleagues, and never have to think twice about.

Based in Baltimore, serving Central Maryland.
