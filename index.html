Our Services

Everything your listing needs to sell faster

A complete media suite built around the demands of real estate. Every deliverable is MLS-optimized and designed to drive showings.

01

Photography

HDR interior & exterior photography with consistent staging awareness and professional retouching.

Every shoot is approached with precision — accurate color, consistent window pulls, and lighting setups that make spaces look their best without misrepresenting them.

Available from 25 to unlimited images. All files delivered at full-resolution and MLS-optimized sizes, labeled and ready for upload.

Book Photography
02

Video Tours

Cinematic walkthroughs that capture flow and feeling no static photo can convey.

Edited highlight reels (60–90 seconds) for social and full walkthrough tours (2–4 minutes) for listing pages. Delivered in 1080p and 4K.

Listings with video consistently see higher engagement on Zillow, Realtor.com, and social media. For competitive properties, it's a real differentiator.

Book Video
03

Drone Aerial

FAA Part 107 certified. Aerial photo and video for properties of all sizes.

Show the lot size, neighborhood context, proximity to water, parks, or amenities — the things buyers care about that ground photography simply can't capture.

All drone flights are FAA compliant and fully insured. We handle airspace authorizations automatically so you don't have to think about it.

About

Built for agents who want a real media partner

Powder River Media is a Baltimore-based real estate media company with a simple focus: deliver professional-quality photography, video, and drone that helps agents compete and win listings.

We're a newer company — and we think that's actually worth saying. It means we're not coasting on reputation. Every shoot matters to us, every client relationship is something we're actively building, and the standard we hold ourselves to reflects that. You'll notice the difference in how we show up and how quickly we deliver.

Based in Baltimore

We cover the greater Baltimore metro area including Baltimore City, Baltimore County, Harford County, Anne Arundel County, and surrounding markets.

Jeff Sellenrick Owner & Photographer
Powder River Media

Jeff Sellenrick

Jeff started Powder River Media because he saw a gap: agents in the Baltimore market deserved better media than what was available at a reasonable price point — and a photographer who actually understood the business of real estate.

His background spans photography, visual storytelling, and a genuine interest in how great media moves property. He approaches every shoot as a collaboration — learning the listing, understanding what needs to be communicated, and making sure the final images reflect the true character of the home.

Jeff is FAA Part 107 certified for drone operations and invests heavily in both gear and technique — because the quality of your listing media reflects on your brand as much as his.

PRM is new, and Jeff believes in being upfront about that. What he can promise is attention, responsiveness, and work that earns the next booking. He's building this business one great shoot at a time — and he'd like yours to be next.
